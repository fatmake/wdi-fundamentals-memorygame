Instrucrions

your task is to find the matching cell. If the next cell you tap on contains the same image, then it’s a match and the two cells remain visible.If the next cell does not contain the same image, then it’s a miss and both cells go back to the hidden state. continue until tyou find all matching cells.


What's fun about it?

you can play it with your friends or your family members and challange them,The one who earns more score points WINS!.And that's how you boost your ADRENALINE.But it's important to be good sport after competition =)!


Why should people play?

  • improve concentration.
  • train visual memory.
  • increase short term memory.
  • increase attention to detail.
  • improve the ability to find similarities and differences in objects.
  • help to classify objects that are grouped by similar traits.
